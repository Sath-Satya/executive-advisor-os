## Q2 Performance — Evidence

**Revenue:** $48.3M &nbsp;▲ 11% YoY &nbsp;|&nbsp; Plan: $47.1M &nbsp;*(+$1.2M ahead)*

**Gross Margin:** 63.4% &nbsp;▲ 180 bps vs. Q1 &nbsp;|&nbsp; Plan: 62.0%

**Net New Customers:** 214 &nbsp;|&nbsp; Plan: 200 &nbsp;*(+7%)*

---

**Three signals driving the outperformance:**

- **Value-based contracts** — 6 renewals closed above list; churn rate held at 3.1%
- **Claims AI module** — deployed to 31 health plans; reduced manual review time 42%
- **Hiring velocity** — 91% of approved headcount filled vs. 74% in Q1

*One miss: Pacific Southwest pipeline conversion at 38% vs. 55% plan. Addressed in slide 3.*

## Decision Required — Pacific Southwest Entry

**Context:** 90-day launch window closes August 31. Two options on the table.

| &nbsp; | Option A — Launch Q3 | Option B — Defer to Q1 2026 |
|---|---|---|
| **Investment** | $4.2M incremental capex | $0 this year |
| **Revenue upside** | $7–9M in first 12 months | Foregone this cycle |
| **Risk** | Competitor launches August 15 | Market position erosion est. 18 months |
| **Recommendation** | **Proceed** | Not recommended |

**Ask:** Approve Option A and authorize $4.2M incremental capex drawdown by June 30.
