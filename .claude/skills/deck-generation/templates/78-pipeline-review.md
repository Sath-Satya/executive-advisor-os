<!-- Speaker notes:
The pipeline review is the most discipline-intensive meeting in the sales calendar. The AE's job is to present an accurate picture of reality — not an optimistic one, and not a pessimistic one. Leadership's job is to challenge assumptions, unblock obstacles, and make resourcing decisions. The 3.3x coverage ratio is healthy for a quarter with a $850K target. Standard enterprise coverage expectation is 3–4x because not every opportunity will close in the intended quarter. Before this meeting, pull your pipeline data from Salesforce and reconcile it with your notes. Every deal should have a next action and a close date — if either is missing, the deal does not belong in a committed forecast. The "Week of April 28" label makes this deck specific and perishable — pipeline reviews are snapshots, not evergreen documents. Date every version.
