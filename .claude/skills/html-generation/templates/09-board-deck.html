Board Action Required

Decisions for Today's Session

Four items require formal board vote.

  • Phoenix Market Entry Contingency Plan — Authorise leased-space option Vote Required

    Authorise CFO to execute 18-month lease on 8,200 sq ft Phoenix site as contingency for permitting delay. Estimated cost $1.4M vs $0.9M permanent site over 18 months. Difference: $500K.

    Ask: Authorise or defer to permanent site + accept 8-week delay?

  • Series C Timeline — Begin investor relationship-building in Q3 2027 Pre-Approved

    Board approved in principle in April 2027. Confirming Q3 start. CEO will brief board monthly on conversation pipeline. Formal raise begins Q4 2027.

    Ask: Confirm CEO authority to conduct non-binding investor conversations through Q3.

  • Revenue Cycle Vendor — Authorise penalty clause activation Vote Required

    Vendor missed Q2 delivery date. Contract allows $250K penalty activation. Legal recommends proceeding to signal credibility. COO recommends deferring to preserve working relationship.

    Ask: Activate penalty clause or accept remediation timeline without penalty?

  • 2028 Capital Budget — Approve $22-26M total spend Q3 Board Meeting

    Full 2028 capital plan deferred to September board meeting pending Phoenix decision and Q3 results. Preliminary range: $22-26M including Series C proceeds.

    No action required today.
