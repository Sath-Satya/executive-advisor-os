Discovery Questions (By Stage)
Stage 1 — Qualification
  • 01What’s your current care gap closure rate, and how does it compare to your state benchmark?
  • 02How are your case managers spending their time today — what percentage is administrative versus direct member engagement?
  • 03Do you have a quality bonus program or value-based arrangement where HEDIS scores directly affect revenue?
  • 04Who owns the member outreach strategy — is it centralized or distributed by program line?
  • 05What does your current outreach stack look like — batch SMS, IVR, manual calling?
Stage 2 — Technical Discovery
  • 06What EHR are you on, and do you have an existing data integration layer or API gateway?
  • 07How do you currently manage member consent and communication channel preferences for HIPAA compliance?
  • 08Who controls your data governance policy — is there a CDO or Chief Compliance Officer we need to engage?
  • 09What does your IT project queue look like for H2 — are there competing infrastructure priorities?
  • 10Have you evaluated any AI-powered outreach platforms before? What happened?

Objection Handling
  • We already have Epic for outreach — why do we need a separate platform?
    Epic gives you the data — SponAI gives you the action engine. Our pre-built Epic connector means you can act on Epic insights through AI-driven outreach without any Epic upgrade. Ask: “How many HEDIS gaps do you close today by exporting a list from Epic and manually calling members?”
  • We’re concerned about AI compliance and HIPAA in member communications.
    We’re SOC 2 Type II and HITRUST certified. Every outreach is governed by your existing consent configuration. Our CDO brief covers the full data governance model — most CDOs approve within 2 weeks. Meridian’s CDO signed off after a single technical review session.
  • Your price is too high — CareConnect quoted us 18% less.
    CareConnect’s standard implementation alone is $40K+ and takes 6 months. Our full implementation is $24K and go-live in 45 days. On a 3-year TCO basis, we’re typically 12–15% less. Can I walk you through the TCO comparison?
  • We don’t have budget this cycle.
    A lot of our payer customers initially said the same thing — and then we showed them how quality bonuses fund the investment. If a 10pp improvement in care gap closure generates $480K in quality bonuses for a plan your size, the ROI positive from Day 91. Let me show you the Meridian Health case study.
