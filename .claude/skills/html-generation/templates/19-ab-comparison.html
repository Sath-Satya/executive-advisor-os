Verdict & Recommendation

◆
Variant B wins on 3 of 4 primary metrics with high confidence

Variant B (Progressive Onboarding) demonstrates statistically significant improvement in completion rate (+15.7pp, 99.2% confidence), Day-7 retention (+7.4pp, 97.1%), and time-to-first-value (−7.2 min, 98.4%). The support ticket reduction is directionally positive but not yet significant at 95%.

Trade-off: Profile completion rate decreased 12.8pp under Variant B due to optional fields. This is acceptable given the primary objective is activation, not data completeness. A follow-up progressive profile-completion nudge should be scoped.

Recommendation: Ship Variant B to 100% of users. Scope profile-completion follow-up as EXP-2026-015. Monitor Day-30 activation for 2 additional weeks.
