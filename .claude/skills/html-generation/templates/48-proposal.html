Executive Summary

Meridian Health Systems processes over 42,000 prior-authorization claims monthly, of which an estimated 34% require manual clinical-staff review due to ambiguous payer rules and incomplete documentation. This backlog creates a 6–11 day average turnaround, contributing to member dissatisfaction scores below the regional average.

SponAI Consulting proposes a three-phase engagement to deploy an AI-assisted claims triage layer integrated directly into Meridian's existing Salesforce Health Cloud environment. Phase 1 establishes the data foundation and model abstraction layer. Phase 2 delivers intelligent routing and auto-approval for clearly compliant claims. Phase 3 introduces a HITL feedback loop and observability dashboard for continuous improvement.

Expected outcomes: 40–55% reduction in manual review queue, 3–5 day improvement in average turnaround, and a measurable lift in first-call resolution for member inquiries.
