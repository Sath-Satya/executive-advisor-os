Expansion Opportunity

Member Engagement Suite — 1,000-Seat License Expansion

Meridian’s Medicaid population health program is expanding to 4 new counties in Q3 2026. The current outreach infrastructure (manual calls + basic batch SMS) cannot scale to the projected 340,000 additional members. The Member Engagement Suite provides AI-assisted outreach scheduling, personalized communication sequencing, and HIPAA-compliant channel management — purpose-built for payer workflows. Priya Nair’s team has already piloted the capability on 50 seats with strong adoption metrics (see Q1 QBR).
