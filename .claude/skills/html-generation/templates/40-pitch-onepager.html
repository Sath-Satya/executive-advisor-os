Problem

Data is locked behind SQL skills

Only 4% of enterprise employees can write SQL. The other 96% submit tickets. Data teams spend 60% of their time on ad-hoc queries instead of strategic work. Average wait: 3.2 business days per question.

Existing BI tools require a data analyst to configure dashboards. When business questions change — and they always do — the dashboard goes stale.

Solution

Any question. Any warehouse. Instant.

Meridian connects to your data warehouse, auto-indexes it, and serves a natural language query interface. Our proprietary Chain-of-Verification engine generates SQL, validates it against schema constraints, executes it read-only, and returns a confidence score with every answer.

  • 40+ native connectors (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift…)
  • 2 minutes from sign-up to first query
  • SOC 2 Type II certified, column-level RBAC
